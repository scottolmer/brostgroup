Tricia Brost

Tricia Brost grew up in Sheboygan, Wisconsin. Well-known in the natural food and supplement industry, she owned and operated Nature’s Best from 1994 through 2015, successfully navigating the business of retail sales and service and growing her store for over 21-years.

An early riser, Tricia always makes the most out of each and every day. She epitomizes integrity, energy, hard work and creative problem solving, from start to finish, through every transaction. A confident and self-motivated leader, Tricia has the uncanny ability to multi-task while still working to get the job done accurately and with attention to detail.

When not diligently and seamlessly guiding clients through all aspects of the home buying and selling process, including relocations, Tricia enjoys all outdoor activities from hiking to tennis and is an avid horse enthusiast and dressage rider.

If you want someone who will stand by you and have your back while buying or selling a home, Tricia is the right choice for you. She is a customer-oriented and strategic sales professional with over 21-years of experience in building relationships, cultivating partnerships and retaining clients.

Jen Hercreg

Jennifer Hercreg orchestrates personalized and rewarding real estate experiences. She offers, quite simply, SINCERE SERVICE, and she will be your most important ally as you buy or sell a home.

As a licensed real estate salesperson with Coldwell Banker Residential Brokerage in Cedarburg, Wisconsin, Jennifer Hercreg facilitates real estate transactions between sellers and buyers. She lists and locates properties, generates market analyses, collaborates with and advises clients, and drafts and negotiates real estate contracts. She is armed with the advanced training and tools provided by Coldwell Banker, including its Featured Agent Branding, through which important leads from the top real estate websites, such as Zillow.com, Realtor.com, Trulia.com and more, reach her directly within seconds. 

On a more specialized and personal level, Jennifer Hercreg brings with her a well rounded and multi-faceted skills set:

- Adept at staging homes, showcasing them effectively so as to entice prospective buyers. 

- Familiar with and has sold homes of widely varying ages, sizes, and architectural styles.

- Enthusiastic and supportive personality that facilitates smooth buying and selling experiences for her clients

As a veteran high school English teacher and college education instructor of 25 years, Jennifer developed talents that have transitioned well into her real estate career:

- Outstanding communication and organizational skills

- A knack for successful collaboration with individuals of diverse backgrounds

- Creative problem-solving

Facilitating an ideal match between buyer and property is an important and rewarding accomplishment, and Jennifer Hercreg takes pride in her ability to do just that. When you are ready to buy or sell your next home, choose Jennifer as your real estate professional.

Wena Roeloffze

Wena Roeloffze, a native of South Africa, presently resides in Wisconsin where she serves as a Destination Specialist and Real Estate Professional. Wena assists relocating families and individuals with the many details of integrating into their new communities. She also has experience as a Professional Organizer, Stager and Concierge Consultant for Corporate Housing companies.

Prior to relocating herself, Wena was a Lawyer and managed a Securities Department for a large Commercial Bank. She is also a Certified Exercise Specialist and Lifestyle Coach and worked for ten years in the Health and Wellness industry helping people overcome chronic diseases.

Wena had the opportunity to travel the world due to working as a Gym Director on a Cruise ship and has international living experience in the UK and Germany. She speaks her native Afrikaans and English fluently and holds two Law degrees, two Fitness certifications and several other certificates ranging from photography to television presenting.

Wena is a well-rounded professional and will do her best to make your transfer to your new home as effortless as possible. Her life motto is: “Do what needs to be done”. Whether you are buying, selling, or leasing, contact her today.
